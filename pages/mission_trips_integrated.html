Our Burkina Faso Journeys

Two transformative mission trips building foundations and expanding impact

Strategic Mission Approach

Our work in Burkina Faso represents a strategic, phased approach to holistic ministry.

Each mission trip built upon the previous one, deepening relationships, expanding impact, and demonstrating Christ's love in practical, powerful ways. From our initial foundation-building trip with 4 team members to our comprehensive multi-disciplinary mission with medical professionals, educators, and builders, we've established lasting partnerships and sustainable ministry.
