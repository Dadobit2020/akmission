Living the Gospel

The Gospel isn't just spoken—it is lived. We serve the whole person: spiritually, physically, and educationally.

At Addis Kidan Evangelical Church Mission Department, we believe in a holistic approach to ministry. Our work in Burkina Faso demonstrates that true transformation happens when we address the complete needs of individuals and communities.
