## üé® Image Guidelines

### Background Images
- Must be high resolution (1920px width minimum)
- Should have good contrast for text overlay
- Will have orange/brown gradient overlay applied
- Landscape orientation

### Content Images
- Good quality (800-1200px width)
- Clear subject matter
- Can be portrait or landscape
- Should tell a story

### Gallery Images
- Consistent sizing within each gallery
- Square or 4:3 aspect ratio preferred
- Clear, well-lit photos
- Diverse representation of activities

### File Format
- JPEG for photos (quality 80-90%)
- PNG for graphics/logos with transparency
- Optimize for web (keep under 500KB per image)
